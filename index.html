The problem

Information has too many doors.

Ideas, tasks, and follow-ups arrive from every direction — a call, a hallway conversation, a late-night thought. Most of it never makes it anywhere durable.

Ideas disappear

A good thought at the wrong moment is gone by morning.

Tasks get forgotten

Whatever isn't written down competes with everything else in your head.

Follow-ups slip through

Commitments made in passing rarely get revisited on their own.

Knowledge stays in one head

Nothing gets easier to hand off if it never leaves your memory.

The solution

One system. Every input.

Universal Capture

Send a text or a voice note. That's the whole interface.

AI Understanding

Every message is classified, summarized, and prioritized automatically.

Operational Memory

Everything lands in one place — organized, searchable, and never lost.
